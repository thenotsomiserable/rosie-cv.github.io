Choosing your programme of study

Complete and submit your application for Postgraduate study

In order to access the online application system, you need to choose a programme of study from the list below.

If you are not sure which programme to choose, please contact the relevant department (postgraduate taught courses) or the Doctoral College (postgraduate research courses).

If you are a Doctoral applicant for the 2019/20 academic year of entry, you should use the relevant link to a programme of study and enter your preferred start date on the application form.

If you have any technical problems, contact the Computing Services servicedesk.
